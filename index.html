Research Interests

  • My research interest is mainly based on logistics model under different uncertainty.
  • To develop new model considering of new factors based on real life situation and to solve them using different type of soft computing techniques and optimization algorithm.
  • To develop some theory to handle different type of uncertainty and their crisp conversion methods.
  • To use different advanced techniques like intelligent transportation system (ITS) in logistic modelling to reduce the environment pollution.
  • To make the transportation system eco-friendly by introducing some new advanced technology.
  • Semi-conductor industry and their mathematical modelling.
  • Mathematical modelling of humanitarian logistics and disaster management and their solution approch.
